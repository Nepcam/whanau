Te Awhipera

Te Awhipera was the daughter of Hinerangi Mahuta of Ngāti Koroki Kahukura, Maungatautari. Te Awhipera married 1st husband, a Millar and later her 2nd husband an Aubrey. Te Awhipera had a son to Aubrey, his name was Paora Aubrey. It is said that Te Awhipera was disowned by her whānau because she married two pākehā. Te Awhipera was the daughter of a high chief who had close ties with the kingitanga. Te Awhipera died at Taupiri at the age of 104.

Mokopurangi Nepe

Born in the Waikato, the second of three sons, Mokopurangi descends from the eldest brother of Te Wherowhero the 1st Māori King. Mokopurangi was arranged to be married to the princess Te Puea Herangi, however, he avoided this by moving to the East Coast. Mokopurangi eventually married Hariata Te Iri of Ngāti Porou. They had 6 sons and 2 daughters. He was also a successful business man apparently owning most of the shops in Ruatoria.
