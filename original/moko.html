Ko Mokopurangi Nepe

Born in the Waikato, the second of three sons, Mokopurangi descends from the eldest brother of Te Wherowhero the 1st Māori King.

Mokopurangi was arranged to be married to the princess Te Puea Herangi, however, he avoided this by moving to the East Coast

 

Mokopurangi eventually married Hariata Te Iri of Ngāti Porou. They had 6 sons and 2 daughters. He was also a successful business man apparently owning most of the shops in Ruatoria.
